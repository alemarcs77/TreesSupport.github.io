Why the Initiatives?

We understand the unique needs of tree growers, corporate social responsibility and green philanthropist.

Planting and tending trees take long years before growers can rip the financial benefits. Those trees they planted sequestered tons of CO2 –  but they don’t get financially compensated for many years of providing carbon offset and benefits to society. While many desires to sponsor a forest for Corporate Social Responsibility, but the lack of dependable technology to track the tree’s development hinders their confidence to engage.

TREE.SUPPORT develops deep learning technology that creates environmental and economic value for tree growers and green philanthropist worldwide.

Our Top Priorities

Tree planting and tending activities are essential factors for trees to thrive. Our top priorities are the overall outcome of these activities for the environment and the people involved in sustainable change. We empower them with simple and reliable technology that helps restore their forests and monitor their impact. TREES.SUPPORT contributes to the achievement of all of the UN’s Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), and notably:

Climate Action

Take urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts.

Life on land

Use existing bio-based resources in a sustainable manner.

No Poverty

Incentives to tree growers can lift local communities out of poverty.

Gender equality

Empower women via tech-transfer and small-enterprises like nurseries.

Decent work

Sponsoring forestry projects to promote work in local communities.

We Join Forces to Impact Our Future, and Together We Disrupt Climate Change  

There is a crucial need for every individual to get involve and make a tangible impact by growing trees, but if you couldn’t plant your own tree(s) then supporting tree growers and forest restoration organizations who help conserve trees in their community is an effective way to help.

Our contributions will enable tree growers to support themselves and their families while revitalizing their local forests resource.

Mission

1 Billion newly planted and mature trees worldwide are geo-tagged by 2025 using deep learning technology we developed.

Our mission is to make sure that tree improvement, long-term maintenance and end-use of the trees is monitored.

We also want to make sure that our technology creates environmental and economic value and can serve as a reliable source of income for millions of smallholder tree growers worldwide.
